Got Questions?

WE'VE GOT
ANSWERS.

Everything you need to know before booking — straight, honest answers. No fluff.

How long will my detail take?＋
It depends on the package and your vehicle's condition. M2 Refresh: 1–2 hrs · M2 Signature: 3–5 hrs · M2 Elite: 6–8 hrs. Larger vehicles or heavily soiled interiors may take longer. We'll give you a time estimate when you drop off.
Do I need to stay while my car is being detailed?＋
Nope — this is a drop-off service. Leave your car with us and go about your day. We'll text or call you as soon as it's ready for pickup. We also offer VIP pickup & drop-off if you'd rather not drive to us at all.
What's the difference between a detail and a regular car wash?＋
A regular car wash is surface-level — water, soap, done. A detail is a thorough, hands-on service that deep cleans, decontaminates, and protects every inch of your vehicle inside and out. No automated tunnels, no shortcuts.
How often should I get my car detailed?＋
For most people, every 3–4 months is ideal. If you drive daily, have kids or pets, or park outside, every 6–8 weeks keeps your vehicle in top condition. We offer monthly maintenance plans to make it effortless.
Can you remove pet hair, stains, or bad odors?＋
Yes — this is one of our specialties. Pet hair removal, stain treatment, and odor elimination are all part of our Signature and Elite packages. Severe cases may require additional time or cost, which we'll always discuss with you upfront before doing anything.
Will detailing remove scratches from my paint?＋
Light swirl marks and minor surface scratches can be improved or removed with paint correction. Deep scratches that go through the clear coat require paint work. We'll inspect your paint and tell you honestly what's achievable — no overpromising.
What if my car needs more work than expected?＋
We'll contact you before doing anything additional. You'll always know the cost before we proceed — no surprises, no hidden charges. Transparency is one of our core values at M2.
Do you detail all vehicle types?＋
Yes — sedans, coupes, SUVs, trucks, vans, and luxury/exotic vehicles. We've detailed everything from daily drivers to Porsches and Range Rovers. Pricing adjusts based on vehicle size.
Why do you require a $25 deposit to book?＋
The $25 deposit is paid instantly when you book — it holds your slot and goes toward your total. No-shows and last-minute cancellations hurt small businesses, and this keeps things fair for everyone. You'll never pay more than quoted.
How do I know which package is right for my car?＋
Just give us a call or send us a text at 972-245-1090 — we'll ask a few quick questions and point you to exactly what your car needs, honestly, without upselling.
